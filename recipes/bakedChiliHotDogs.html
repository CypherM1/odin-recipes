Baked Chili Hot Dogs

Description

Below you will see the directions on how to make baked chili hot dogs.

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 9 x 13-inch baking dish.
  2. Step 2: PHeat hot dogs according to package directions. Arrange hot dog buns in baking dish, cut side up. Place hot dogs in buns. Top with chili. Sprinkle with cheese. Tent pan with foil.
  3. Step 3: Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until hot and cheese is melted.
